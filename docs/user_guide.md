# User Guide: Location-Based Anonymous Chat Application

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to the Local Anonymous Chat App Blimp! This application allows you to connect with people nearby in real-time without revealing your personal identity. While you log in securely using your Google account, your chat persona remains completely anonymous.

---

## 2. Getting Started & Logging In

### Step 1: Access the Application
* Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, or Edge).
* Navigate to the application URL : `https://proximity-chat-app-project-3-7e31e19bd283.herokuapp.com`

### Step 2: Login with Google
* On the landing page, click the button labeled **"Sign in with Google"**.
* A pop-up window or redirect will take you to Google’s secure login page.
* Select your Google account and enter your password if prompted.
* **Note:** This step is only to verify you are a real person and are a tamu student. Your email address and real name will **never** be shown to other users in the chat.

---

## 3. Location & Privacy Setup

### Step 1: Grant Location Permissions
* Upon your first login, the browser will ask for permission to access your location.
* Click **"Allow"** when prompted.
* **Why is this needed?** The application uses your GPS coordinates to place you in a "Lobby" specific to your current area. You will only see chats relevant to your physical location.

### Step 2: Your Anonymous Identity
* Once logged in, the system will assign you a temporary **Anonymous Display Name** (e.g., *NeonTiger*, *BlueFalcon*, or *Guest_123*).
* Other users will see this nickname, not your Google Name.

---

## 4. Using the Chat Application

### Sending Messages
* Type your message in the text box at the bottom of the chat screen.
* Press **Enter** or click the **Send** arrow.
* **Real-Time Updates:** Your message will appear instantly to everyone else in that room without needing to refresh the page.

### Receiving Messages
* Incoming messages from other users will appear in the chat stream instantly.
* Messages are color-coded to help distinguish between your messages and those from others.

---

## 5. Logging Out

### Secure Logout
* To leave the application, click the **"Logout"** button usually located in the top-right corner of the screen.
* This will:
    1.  End your active session.
    2.  Disconnect you from the real-time chat server.
    3.  Redirect you back to the main Login page.

---

## 6. Troubleshooting
* **"My messages aren't sending."**
    * Check your internet connection.
    * If the issue persists, try refreshing the page to reconnect to the chat server.

* **"Is my email visible?"**
    * No. Your Google account is strictly used for authentication (logging in). Your public profile in the chat is completely anonymized.
